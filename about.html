About Us

Nestled in the heart of Pine City, Pine City Mall is your premier shopping destination, offering a diverse range of retail, dining, and entertainment experiences. Our mall is designed to be more than just a place to shop; it's a vibrant community hub where you can enjoy a day out with friends and family.

Our Story

Pine City Mall was founded in July of 2018 with a vision to create a premier shopping destination that reflects the spirit of Pine City. Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to enhancing the community by offering a space where people can come together, shop, dine, and unwind.

Our Mission:

At Pine City Mall, our mission is to create an inviting and dynamic environment that caters to the needs and desires of our visitors. We strive to provide exceptional customer service, a diverse selection of stores, and a range of amenities to ensure a memorable experience every time you visit.

What We Offer:

  • Retail Variety: Explore a wide array of stores, from high-end fashion boutiques to popular brand retailers. Whether you're searching for the latest trends or unique finds, Pine City Mall has something for everyone.
  • Dining Delights: Savor delicious meals at our selection of restaurants and food courts. From gourmet dining to quick bites, our diverse food options cater to every taste and preference
  • Entertainment & Events: Enjoy a range of entertainment options, including movie theatres and seasonal events. Stay tuned to our events calendar to catch all the exciting happenings at the mall.
  • Convenience & Comfort: Our mall is designed with your convenience in mind, featuring ample parking, comfortable seating areas, and family-friendly amenities. We aim to provide a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience.

Visit US

Located at 346 Pine Street, Pine City Mall is easily accessible and welcomes visitors from near and far. Our friendly staff and inviting ambiance make every visit enjoyable. We invite you to come and experience everything we have to offer.
