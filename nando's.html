Stores Restaurants Nando's

"Welcome to Nando's, where flavour takes flight and every meal is a culinary adventure. Prepare your taste buds for a journey of bold spices and succulent flame-grilled chicken, expertly marinated in our legendary PERi-PERi sauce. Whether you're craving our tender chicken thighs, juicy burgers, or hearty wraps, Nando's promises a burst of flavour that's uniquely South African and undeniably addictive. With our vibrant atmosphere and commitment to quality ingredients, Nando's invites you to ignite your senses and celebrate the art of good food with friends and family. Join us and discover why Nando's is more than a meal—it's a deliciously unforgettable experience."

Nando's was founded in 1987. The first Nando's restaurant was opened in Rosettenville, Johannesburg, South Africa. Since then, Nando's has grown into an international casual dining restaurant chain known for its Portuguese-style peri-peri chicken dishes. The centrepiece of their menu is marinated chicken, grilled to perfection with a spicy peri-peri sauce derived from African bird's eye chili peppers. With over 1,000 restaurants across six continents, Nando's offers a diverse menu including burgers, wraps, and sides like peri-peri fries. Known for its vibrant and culturally resonant branding, Nando's combines flavourful cuisine with a casual dining experience, emphasizing community engagement and a playful approach to celebrating South African heritage through its global presence.

One unique aspect of Nando's is its commitment to sourcing and preparing quality ingredients, particularly its signature peri-peri sauce. Nando's uses African bird's eye chili peppers (peri-peri peppers) in its sauces, which are known for their distinctively spicy and flavourful characteristics. The peri-peri sauce is a key component of Nando's menu, offering customers a range of heat levels to suit their preferences. This dedication to using authentic ingredients and creating a unique flavour experience has helped Nando's differentiate itself in the competitive restaurant industry, both in South Africa and globally. Additionally, Nando's playful and culturally relevant marketing approach, often incorporating humour and social commentary, has contributed to its strong brand identity and popularity among diverse audiences worldwide.
