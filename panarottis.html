Stores Restaurants PANAROTTIS

"Experience the taste of Italy at Panarottis, where every bite brings you closer to the heart of authentic Italian cuisine. Indulge in our freshly made pizzas, crafted with love and topped with the finest ingredients. From classic Margheritas to gourmet combinations bursting with flavour, each pizza is a masterpiece. Pair your meal with our delectable pasta dishes, salads, and irresistible desserts for a complete Italian dining experience. With a warm and inviting atmosphere, Panarottis is the perfect place to gather with friends and family, share laughter, and enjoy food that comforts and satisfies. Join us at Panarottis and discover why we're passionate about bringing Italy to your table."

Panarottis was founded in 1990 in Parow, a suburb of Cape Town, South Africa. Since then, Panarottis has expanded its presence across South Africa and also operates internationally, offering Italian-style pizza and pasta dishes in a family-friendly dining atmosphere, focusing focus on quality ingredients and a wide variety of menu options that cater to different tastes and preferences. Emphasizing quality ingredients and traditional flavours, Panarottis offers a diverse menu that caters to various tastes, from classic pizzas with a range of toppings to hearty pasta dishes and fresh salads. Known for its family-friendly atmosphere, Panarottis provides a welcoming environment where families and friends can gather to enjoy delicious meals and create lasting memories. With a growing presence both locally and internationally, Panarottis continues to uphold its commitment to delivering exceptional Italian cuisine and fostering community engagement wherever it operates.

One unique aspect of Panarottis is its signature pizza offering, known as the "Big Sausage Roll" pizza. This specialty pizza is distinctive for its rolled crust filled with a savoury blend of cheese and herbs, encasing a generous portion of sausage. This innovative creation sets Panarottis apart in the pizza landscape, offering customers a unique and indulgent dining experience that combines traditional Italian flavours with a creative twist. Additionally, Panarottis' commitment to maintaining a family-friendly environment and consistently high-quality ingredients across its menu enhances its appeal as a favourite dining destination for Italian cuisine enthusiasts and families alike.
